Privacy Policy for Tabela

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Thank you for using Tabela. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is handled when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Tabela is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information (PII), browsing history, IP addresses, or usage analytics to any external servers.

2. Local Storage

Our extension functions locally on your device. We use your browser's standard Local Storage functionality solely to save your specific application preferences (such as your favorited "starred" football matches and notification settings). This data remains strictly on your local machine and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

In order to provide real-time football scores, match events, and fixtures, Tabela makes external network requests directly to ESPN's public sports APIs (site.api.espn.com). These requests are solely for fetching public sports data and do not include or transmit any of your personal information.

4. Permissions

Tabela requires specific browser permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage: To save your favorite matches locally.
  • Alarms & Background: To periodically check for live score updates of your favorite matches in the background.
  • Notifications: To send you desktop alerts when a goal is scored or a match event changes.
  • Host Permissions: To fetch the live JSON score data from the ESPN API.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy or the Chrome extension, please contact the developer via the official Chrome Web Store support mechanisms or through my official portfolio website.
